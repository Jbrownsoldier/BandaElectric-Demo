# Design System: Hero: Trust & Precision Specialist
**Project ID:** 8658139791597780561

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Creative North Star: "The Precision Conduit"**
The design system adopts an **Editorial Precision** approach, viewing electrical infrastructure as a sophisticated, high-tech element of modern life. It features high-contrast tonal shifts, intentional asymmetry, and rhythmic spacing, creating a premium architectural aesthetic that conveys authority and meticulous engineering.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
The palette is anchored by deep, oceanic teals and energized by high-voltage gold.

*   **Deep Ocean Foundation (Primary: #001f34):** The foundation for high-authority backgrounds and deep immersion.
*   **The Spark (Secondary/Accent: #ffb219):** Reserved for critical calls to action and highlighting precision details.
*   **Urgent Alert (Tertiary: #f24236):** Used sparingly for critical status or urgent information.
*   **Precision Surface (Surface: #f7fafd):** The clean, neutral background for the entire application.
*   **Deep Immersion (Primary Container: #003554):** Used for large grounding areas like footers.

## 3. Typography Rules
The system balances technical precision with human-centric clarity.

*   **Editorial Headlines (Work Sans):** Bold weights and wide tracking are used for "Editorial Statements." Large scales (up to 3.5rem) command immediate attention in hero sections.
*   **Technical Data & Body (Inter):** A neutral, highly readable counterpoint used for all body text and data presentation.
*   **Visual Rhythm:** Hierarchy is established through high-contrast pairings (e.g., Primary Display text with Secondary Title labels).

## 4. Elevation & Depth
The system eschews traditional drop shadows in favor of **Tonal Layering**.

*   **Tonal Layering:** Depth is achieved by stacking surface tiers. Elements move "higher" by shifting towards pure white (#ffffff) against darker backgrounds.
*   **Ambient Shadows:** For floating elements, a soft shadow tinted with the primary hue is used: `rgba(0, 31, 52, 0.08)` with 32px blur.
*   **The "No-Line" Rule:** 1px solid borders are strictly prohibited. Boundaries are defined through background color shifts and tonal nesting.

## 5. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons (Power Units):**
    *   **Primary:** Solid Secondary Gold (#ffb219) with a 0.5rem corner radius and a subtle inner-glow gradient.
    *   **Secondary:** White background with a "Ghost Border" (outline-variant at 15% opacity).
    *   **Tertiary:** Text-only in Primary Teal with increased letter spacing.
*   **Cards:** Minimalist containers using surface shifts instead of dividers. Interactive cards shift tonal backgrounds on hover.
*   **Input Fields:** Minimalist design with a surface-container background and a 2px Secondary Gold left-accent bar on focus.

## 6. Layout Principles
*   **Asymmetrical Balance:** Layouts often feature offset headlines and text to create dynamic visual interest.
*   **White Space as Structure:** Large gaps are used as structural elements to separate content rather than using lines or dividers.
*   **Grounding:** Large footer areas use deep primary containers to anchor the page.
